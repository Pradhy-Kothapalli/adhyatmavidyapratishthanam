About the Organization

Adhyatma Vidya Pratishthanam supports spiritual learning through satsangs, discussions, pravachanas, and religious events. The goal is to encourage dharmic living and share Vedantic teachings in a practical and accessible way.

Activities

Spiritual, religious, and Vedantic teachings through community gatherings.

Events

Programs such as Akhanda Bhagavata Maha Yagna and mantra japa initiatives.

Digital Presence

Zoom and YouTube sessions so devotees can join from anywhere.
