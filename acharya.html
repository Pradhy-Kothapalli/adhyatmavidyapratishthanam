Acharyaru

Sri Maligi Vidyanidhi Acharya is a distinguished scholar of Dwaitha Vedantha and a devoted preacher of Madhwa philosophy. He has led several spiritual initiatives including the first Dashakoti Krishna Mantra Yagam in 2017.

Education

  • MA in Sahitya - Rashtriya Sanskrit Vidyapeetha
  • M.Phil in Dwaitha Vedantha - Rashtriya Sanskrit Vidyapeetha
  • Ph.D in Dwaitha Vedantha - National Sanskrit University (NSU)

Honors & Achievements

Gold Medalist in Dwaitha Vedantha for 2 consecutive years at the Sri Venkateswara Veda Vignana Sadassu conducted by Dharmagiri, Tirumala, along with excellence in other Shastras.

Major Spiritual Initiative

First Dashakoti Krishna Mantra Yagam conducted in 2017.

Programs & Spiritual Services

Pandit Vidyanidhi Achar also conducts Pravachana, Sri Sitarama Kalyanam, Sri Srinivasa Kalyanam and performs Dasakoti Krishna Mantra Japa Parayana and Homa by giving an opportunity for all Hindus irrespective of their age groups from past 9 years. It has been a great success.

  • The Homa was led by Sri Pujya Vidhyadheesacharya Guttal in the 1st year.
  • The Homa was led by Sri Pujya Kheda Krishnachar in the 2nd year, and continued similarly in later years.
