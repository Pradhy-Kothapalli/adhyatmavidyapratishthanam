Acharyaru Profile

Sri Dr. Vidyanidhi Achar

Sri Dr. Vidyanidhi Achar is one of the eminent disciples evolved from Satyadhyana Vidyapeetha, Mumbai, under the guidance of Sri Mahuli Acharyaru. He has studied and completed the texts Sudhanta Vedantha and Sriman Nyayasudha, and has taken up the responsibilities of Mathadhikari in Kadapa, Sri Uttaradi Mutt.

He has been actively enlightening the glory of Madhwa philosophy, especially by conducting pravachanas on auspicious days such as Prathama Ekadashi, Karthika Ekadashi, and Vaikunta Ekadashi.

Programs & Initiatives

  • Conducts Sri Sitarama Kalyanam and Sri Srinivasa Kalyanam.
  • Organizes Dasakoti Krishna Mantra Japa Parayana and Homa.
  • The Homa is led by Sri Kheda Krishnacharya and fellow pandits.
  • Started a magazine titled “Adhyatma Sudha” for spiritual enrichment.
