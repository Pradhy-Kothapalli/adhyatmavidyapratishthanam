AVP Kadapa — Objectives

Objectives

  • To promote Madhwa culture.
  • To develop the Madhwa community in the fields of social, educational, health, and economical well-being.
  • To spread awareness about Sri Uttaradi Mutt and its traditions.
  • To support the preaching and teachings guided by His Holiness Sri Satyatma Teertharu, the present Pontiff of Uttaradi Mutt.
