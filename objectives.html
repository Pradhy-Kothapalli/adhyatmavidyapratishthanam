Objectives - Adhyatma Vidya Pratishthanam

Objectives of AVP

To promote Madhwa culture and contribute to the holistic development of the Madhwa community in the fields of social, educational, health, economic, and spiritual well-being. The organization also aims to inculcate the teachings and philosophy of His Holiness Sri 1008 Satyatma Teertharu, the present pontiff of Uttaradi Mutt.
